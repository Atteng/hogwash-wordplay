# hogwash-wordplay
The only vibecoded multiplayer crossword game where your IQ meets competitive vocabulary.

Real time, strategic and themed for Superform & Based_Piggy

Why Piggy Themed? Because everything is better with bacon-flavored puns

Can be played solo or by friends, family, and even enemies looking for a good time

Features:
- Multi-Modes: 

--Single Player: Practice, Achieve Englightenment and Conquest

--Competitive: Players compete against each other and time to finish a crossword

--Co-op: Players cooperate to complete a puzzle and beat the time

--Teams: Take competition to the next level, partner up and prove your superior vocabulary

- Multiplayer: Upto 30 Players Supported

- Custom Words/Clues Supported: Play custom crosswords based on what you like/want

- Strategic: Conquer the board, Race against time, Use Hints wisely, avoid penalties, and prove your knowledge is supreme.
